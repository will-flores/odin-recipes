Rice

Description

Rice is a staple diet for most of the world. One of the most versatile ingredient, it can be cooked as an entree, mains or a dessert! Did you know that there are more than 40,000 varieties of rice?

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Garlic
  • Olive Oil
  • Whole Black Pepper

Steps

  1. Mince garlic clove
  2. Add minced garlic, olive oil, and 5 whole black pepper to pot
  3. Add one cup of rice
  4. Add two cups of water
  5. Cook over medium heat for 12 minutes
  6. Serve
